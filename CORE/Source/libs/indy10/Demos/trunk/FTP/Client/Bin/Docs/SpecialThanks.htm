It takes a lot of time and energy to put together the Demo applications that ship with Indy.  Without the aid of some 3rd parties they would not be what they are today.  It is to these persons that we give a special thanks and note about their contribution to the project and any information related to their contribution.

 

Atozed Software (http://www.atozed.com/) – Atozed hosts the News Groups and 3rd party pages associated with Indy.  Without their hosting and contributions it is hard to say where Indy would be now.  They market, distribute and license developer tools and consumer software products.

 

GlyFx (http://www.glyfx.com/) – GlyFx was nice enough to let us bend their standard license agreement on their FreeWare sample image packages and place them into our demos to aid in a professional look and feel.  Please check out their site, their graphics are top notch, and their company is willing to help.  You must abide by their license completely and not redistribute the images found in the Indy Demos.
